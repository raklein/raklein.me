--- title: "Many Labs 2 Under Review" author: "Rick Klein" date: '2018-03-09' slug: manylabs2-nearlydone-rmd categories: ["Updates"] tags: ["many labs 2", "replication", "crowdsourcing"] ---

The Many Labs 2 project (https://osf.io/8cd4r/) has been submitted and is under review at AMPPS. Overall, 28 psychology studies were replicated across >120 samples from 34 countries around the world, totalling >15,000 participants.

Public release of the results/manuscript/data should occur shortly, pending final revisions and alongside commentaries from original authors.
