Many Labs 2 Under Review

 May 5, 2018
The Many Labs 2 project (https://osf.io/8cd4r/) has been submitted and is under review at AMPPS. Overall, 28 psychology studies were replicated across >120 samples from 34 countries around the world, totalling >15,000 participants. Public release of the results/manuscript/data should occur shortly, pending final revisions and alongside commentaries from original authors.
